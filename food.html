🍜 Food Guide

Family of 4 · 2 vegetarians · 2 no-fish · Mom loves noodles / Asian food
Best cuisines for this group: Thai, Italian/pizza, Mexican, Chinese, Mediterranean.
Avoid: heavy seafood places, BBQ-only, steakhouses.
By Day
Monday · Springdale / Zion
Thai Sapa

Best pick Thai and Asian cuisine right in Springdale. Great shot at noodles for mom. Good for vegetarians.

Spotted Dog Cafe Classic Known Springdale spot with local feel.

Bit & Spur Classic Southwestern, local vibe, vegetarian-friendly.

Fallback Pizza / Italian if everyone is too tired to think.

Tuesday · Page, AZ
Thai Chili 2 Go

Best pick Thai with explicit vegetarian and vegan options. Good noodle options for mom.

Chothika Thai Cuisine — Another Thai option in Page.

Backup Mexican in Page for veggie burritos / fajitas / bean dishes.

Wednesday · Cameron, AZ
Cameron Trading Post Restaurant

Classic Most iconic roadside stop of the trip. Keep food expectations modest but experience-wise it's uniquely Southwestern.

Pro tip: Do a bigger lunch earlier and keep expectations realistic.

Thursday · Grand Canyon / Tusayan
We Cook Pizza & Pasta

Best pick Easiest family-friendly option. Vegetarian options, pasta/salad bar. Good practical choice.

Plaza Bonita — Mexican. Works well for vegetarians and the non-fish eaters.

El Tovar Dining Room Classic Historic, iconic, inside the park. The most special dining experience near the canyon. May benefit from a reservation.

Friday · Las Vegas
Lotus of Siam

Classic Arguably the most famous Thai restaurant in Las Vegas. James Beard-recognized. This is the night to give mom a proper noodle meal. May benefit from a reservation.

Peinto Thai Kitchen Practical Fast casual Thai, anything can be made vegetarian. Drunken noodles recommended.

🌱 Vegetarian notes
  • Both Thai restaurants (Sapa, Thai Chili 2 Go, Chothika, Peinto, Lotus of Siam) have good veggie options
  • Pizza + pasta always works — vegetarian pasta, marinara, veggie pizza
  • Mexican works too — veggie burritos, bean dishes, fajitas
  • Tournament of Kings (if you go) has a vegetarian menu — request at least 4 hours ahead
🛒 Grocery stops
  • Monday morning (Vegas) — main stock-up:
    SF Supermarket — Asian groceries / noodle cups · ~15–20 min detour
    Walmart Supercenter — general stock-up · ~10–15 min detour
  • Tuesday (Page) — best mid-trip refill: water, fruit, snacks, breakfast items, ice
  • Cameron: not a good grocery stop — stock up in Page instead
