## 🎯 Activities

| Day | Activity | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Apr 6 | Zion National Park | Take shuttle — no cars in canyon Apr–Nov. Angels Landing or Emerald Pools good for families |
| Apr 7 | Lake Powell / Page area | Wahweap Marina viewpoint, Glen Canyon Dam overlook |
| Apr 8 | Lower Antelope Canyon | Ken's Tours Deluxe. No tripods, no large bags. Dust everywhere — protect phone/camera |
| Apr 8 | Horseshoe Bend | Short 1.5-mile round trip hike. Go early to beat crowds. Fenced overlook. |
| Apr 9 | Grand Canyon South Rim | Mather Point (sunrise/sunset), Bright Angel Trail (take it easy with kids), Rim Trail (easy, paved) |
| Apr 10 | Omega Mart / AREA15 + Vegas fun | Book ahead online at meowwolf.com. Plan 2–3 hours, then explore Vegas after. |

**💡 Tips:**
- **America the Beautiful Pass** ($90) covers Zion + Grand Canyon — buy at first park gate or online at usgs.gov/passes
- Pack snacks for Page/Cameron — limited food options
- April mornings at the canyon can be cold (35–45°F) — layer up
- Antelope Canyon: small bag or pockets only, no large backpacks
